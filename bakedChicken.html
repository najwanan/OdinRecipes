Herbed Baked Chicken

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breasts
  • flour
  • milk
  • egg
  • rosemary
  • salt
  • pepper
  • Thyme
  • butter

Recipe Steps

  1. Butterfly chicken breasts, pound flat and set aside
  2. Prep the wet and dry batter. Wet batter is the egg wash and milk. The dry batter is just the flour and spices
  3. Use one hand for the dry batter and one hand for the wet, dip each chicken cutlet into the wet batter and then the dry
  4. Heat up butter in the pan, toss in sprig of rosemary
  5. Pan fry chicken until golden brown, flip over as needed
  6. Baste chicken with butter
  7. Take off heat, and top with mixture of chopped herbs
