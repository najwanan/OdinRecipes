Mac and Cheese

  • Macaroni
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Shredded Cheddar Cheese
  • Grated Parmesan
  • Thyme
  • Breadcrumbs
    1. Heat up a pan. Make a roux with butter and flour. While this is going, boil your macaroni
    2. Once the flour has cooked and the roux is a golden brown color, slowly add in the milk
    3. Add in your cheeses little by little and stir well to combine
    4. Add in salt, pepper and thyme and the al dente macaroni
    5. This step is optional but to get a crispy top, put your mac and cheese into a baking dish, top with breadcrumbs and butter, and broil in oven for 5 min.
