Ingredients

  • Butternut Squash
  • Tri-colored Radishes
  • Carrots
  • Sweet Potatoes
  • Salt
  • Olive Oil
  • Rosemary
  • Sagee

Recipe Steps

  1. Cut up veggies into similar size pieces
  2. Toss in baking dish to coat with oil
  3. Chop up herbs, use whatever you like or have on hand
  4. Pre-heat oven to 400 deg.
  5. Bake for 30 minutes, tossing veggies halfway through
